Dr. Rowena Cristina L. Guevara

"Women make amazing engineers and leaders when given the opportunity, and when they pursue the opportunity."
Engineer. Changemaker.
Dr. Rowena Guevarra holds a BS Electrical Engineering and MSEE degrees from UP Diliman in 1985 and 1990 respectively, and a PhDEE from the University of Michigan in 1997. She continued her post-doctoral degree in the Tokyo Institute of Technology for Speech Signal Processing in 1998. For more information on her background, checkthisout!
Her area of specialization is speech and audio signal processing, time-frequency analysis and synthesis, and she initiated the Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Laboratory in UPD. Some of her researches includes titles such as:
  • "Filipino sign language recognition using manifold projection learning",
  • "Experiments on automatic language identification for Philippine languages using acoustic Gaussian Mixture Models",
  • "Feature set for Philippine Gong Music classification by indigenous group",
andmany more!
Dr. Rowena Guevarra is the Undersecretary for Research and Development of the Department of Science and Technology (DOST), and a professor in the Electrical and Electronics Engineering Institute (EEEI) in the University of the Philippines (UP) Diliman. As of September 2022, she was appointed as the president of the National Transmission Corp. (TransCo).

She served as the first woman Dean of the UP College of Engineering and has been the recipient of several awards in engineering and education, including the Metrobank Foundation Search for Outstanding Teachers of the Philippines.
